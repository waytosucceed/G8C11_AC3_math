Shirley is a dedicated market vendor who sells a variety of fresh fruits. One day, she buys a new shipment of fruits. She decides to learn more about decimals to price her fruits accurately. Shirley practices calculating the costs and change for her customers.

Shirley, armed with decimal skills, prices fruits precisely by calculating costs per kilogram. Converting percentages to decimals aids in applying discounts accurately, ensuring fair transactions. Her adeptness in rounding off decimals guarantees exact change, leaving customers pleased with her fresh produce and reliable service.
